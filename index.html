At 54, I was overweight, exhausted, and going through a divorce. Every morning I walked into the same bathroom and saw a version of myself I barely recognised.

I wasn't looking for a six-pack. I just wanted to feel like myself again.

So I made one decision: show up. One workout. One clean meal. One day at a time.

Twelve months later — everything changed.
